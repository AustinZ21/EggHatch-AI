# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want EggHatch-AI to be a welcoming project for people interested in AI agents, recommendation systems, and practical product demos. Contributors are expected to communicate with respect, assume good intent, and keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give clear technical feedback without personal attacks.
- Credit others' ideas and contributions.
- Keep issues and pull requests relevant to the project.
- Be patient with contributors who are learning.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, trolling, or unrelated promotion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

If you need to report a concern, open a private communication channel with the maintainer through the contact information listed on the maintainer's GitHub profile.
